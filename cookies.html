Pecan, Raisin and cinammon cookies

Prep time: 30 min

yield: 5 and half

what you will need

how to prepare

  1. Cream butter in a large mixing bowl; gradually add sugar,
    beating until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time,
    beating well after each addition. Add coffee, stirring well
  2. Combine flour, soda, cinnamon, and allspice in a large mixing bowl.
    Stir in raisins and pecans to coat well; add to creamed mixture
  3. Drop dough by heaping teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto greased cookie sheets.
    Bake at 350Â° for 8 minutes. Remove from cookie sheets, and cool on wire racks
