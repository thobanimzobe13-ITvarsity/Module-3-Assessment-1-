Strawberry Milkshake

Prep time: 10 min

yield: 4

what you will need

how to prepare

  1. Blend half of strawberries, 1/4 tsp. vanilla,
    1 cup ice cream and 2 Tbsp.
    milk in blender until smooth
  2. Pour into 2 glasses. Repeat with remaining strawberries,
    vanilla, ice cream and milk. Serve immediately
